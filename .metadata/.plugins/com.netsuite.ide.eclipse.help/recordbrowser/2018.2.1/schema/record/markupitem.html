nonPosting boolean 0..1 Non-posting F Select Non-posting if this discount item should not post to a general ledger account. When a discount item is marked non-posting, it does not post as an individual transaction line. The item it is applied to posts the net amount after the discount. When you create a sales transaction and add the non-posting discount after a line-item, the discount is applied to the previous line-item only. * If a 10% non-posting discount is applied to a $100 line item, the amount of revenue that posts to the associated revenue account for that item is $90. * If a 10% posting discount is applied to a $100 line item, the amount of revenue that posts to the associated revenue account for that item is $100. Also, an offsetting debit amount of $10 posts to the related discount account, such as the Sales Discounts account. For Non-posting discounts, also select an Account the discount amount posts to.
