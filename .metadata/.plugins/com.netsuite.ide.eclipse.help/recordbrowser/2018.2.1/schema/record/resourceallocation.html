approvalStatus ResourceAllocationApprovalStatus 0..1 Approval Status When you use SuiteFlow for resource allocation approvals, the forms now include an Approval Status field. The Approval Status field reflects the state of the transaction in the approval process. Resource allocations may have one of the following statuses: * Pending Approval &#x2013; The allocation is in the approval queue. * Approved &#x2013; The allocation has been given approval for processing. * Rejected &#x2013; The allocation has not been approved and will not be processed.
